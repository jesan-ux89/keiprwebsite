How Keipr Works

Three steps to paycheck clarity

1

Tell Us When You Get Paid

Set your pay schedule — biweekly, weekly, twice monthly, or monthly. Keipr builds your budget around YOUR pay dates, not arbitrary calendar months.

2

Add Your Expenses

Enter your bills manually or connect your bank to auto-detect recurring expenses. Keipr maps each bill to the right paycheck based on its due date.

3

See What's Left

Your dashboard shows exactly how much you can spend after every payday. Track payments, plan months ahead, and split big bills across paychecks.
